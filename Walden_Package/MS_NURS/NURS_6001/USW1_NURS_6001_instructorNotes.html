Week 1

You can utilize additional resources with students that are struggling with APA formatting in their assignments. You may strongly encourage students to use these resources as added aids to be prescribed by the faculty for students with struggling writing issues.

Students are required to work through the Academic Integrity for Undergraduates modules provided by the Writing Center. There is a quiz that may be completed to demonstrate they have learned the materials. The quiz also provides students with additional practice on using APA formatting and recognizing plagiarism. If students are struggling with APA formatting, you may encourage them to complete the quiz that is available.

In addition, you may ask students to complete the School of Nursing Scavenger Hunt. This document asks students to visit specific Walden resources to locate answers pertaining to APA formatting and academic integrity.

Both the quiz and the Scavenger Hunt are available in this week’s Optional Resources. Please click on the following link to receive the answers to the SON Scavenger Hunt:

Week 6

COURSE GRADE: Students in N6001, Foundations of Graduate Study, will receive S (Satisfactory) or U (unsatisfactory) grade based on the weighted total percent shown in the grade area. Enter a grade of “S” for a weighted total percent of 80% or higher. Enter a grade of “U” for a weighted total percent of less than 80%.

NOTE: For each student, a numerical value (0 to 100) needs to be entered into each weekly discussion and assignment/quiz in order for the weighted total percent to be accurate. Prior to inserting a “S” or a “U” letter grade, ensure that the quiz, assignment, and all discussions have a numerical value entered for each student.
